## How this hub works

- Content lives under `content/docs/` as MDX (Fumadocs collections).
- NQChart pages can be refreshed from the sibling becocharts repo with `pnpm docs:sync:nqchart`.
- Old `/readme` URLs redirect here (`/readme` → `/docs/nqui`, `/readme/nqchart` → `/docs/nqchart`).

Pick a library from the top bar or sidebar to get started.
