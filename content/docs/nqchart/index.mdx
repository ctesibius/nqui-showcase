`@nqlib/nqchart` is the chart library for BI dashboards and analytics surfaces. Install primitives via the shadcn registry, compose `NQ*Chart` children, and ship histograms, funnels, gauges, and more.

## Why NQChart?

- **Composable primitives** — `NQBarChart`, `NQLineChart`, and siblings with child parts, not boolean props on one mega chart
- **BI-ready recipes** — histogram, Pareto, waterfall, funnel, gauge helpers in [chart-recipes](/docs/nqchart/chart-recipes)
- **ECharts engine** — canvas performance and rich chart types, with shadcn-aligned tokens and tooltips

Related: [nqui](/docs/nqui) for app chrome.
